Who are you
Who are you
Supreme Associates Traders is an integrated service provider specializing in design, fabrication, installation, and maintenance services, particularly in water treatment solutions. Recognized as a reliable partner, Supreme ensures your water treatment system operates efficiently, allowing you to concentrate on your core activities. Their team of certified professionals, boasting over 20 years of experience, provides comprehensive, simplified, and integrated solutions to help customers optimize their existing systems and assets without incurring additional costs or risks. This commitment to practical, economic, and sustainable solutions underscores Supreme’s dedication to protecting the world's most precious resource—water.
With 12 years of market presence, Supreme Associates Traders offers a one-stop shop for all home appliance needs, including water purifiers, mineral water plants, rainwater collection systems, vacuum cleaners, electric kitchen chimneys, solar water heaters, and car wash facilities. They address all water-related concerns, from drinking water purifiers to bore well drilling, ensuring safe water regardless of the source. Their expertise extends to various areas such as water treatment systems, effluent treatment, sewage treatment, reverse osmosis, industrial and municipal wastewater treatment, and chlorination systems. Supreme's goal is to provide high-quality goods and services that deliver value and satisfaction to their customers.
Our Vision
Our Vision
Our vision is providing sustainable water treatment solutions for a better environment and is to have long relationship with our customers by providing reliable after sales service.
Philosophy
Philosophy
Our objective is to provide access to clean water, as well as to secure environmentally-compatible disposal of municipal and industrial wastewater. We are deeply and strongly committed to the sustainable contribution to environmental protection as well as in the enhancement of the quality of life. This ultimate task steers us in the development of new and innovative technologies and the ongoing optimization of all the existing processes with a focus on emerging as well as developing markets. The success of our efforts directly contributes to securing one of Earth’s greatest and precious treasures, the very basis of life — the element called “Water”.
Our Mission
Our Mission
Our mission is to make the world’s water treatment systems affordable, sustainable as well as energy-efficient in order to cope with the challenges of population growth, industrial expansion and climate change. We supply good service, good quality and good price and we will make unremitting efforts to provide customers with satisfied products and service.
Relationship
Relationship
Through the years, we have gained immense confidence in our clients. We have built lasting relationships with these clients based on our credential of trust as well as innovations. Supreme represents a globally present, locally driven business organization in the field of water treatment technology.
