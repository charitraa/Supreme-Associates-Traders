A WEBINAR ON THE IMPORTANCE OF CHLORINATION AND ITS METHOD

August 20, 2022

image/blog/webniar.jpg" alt="Wastewater treatment process">

24th Nov, 2023 , we held a webinar for the first time to emphasize on the importance and requirement of chlorination. To remove the microorganisms present in water some amount of chlorine is required to add in the water.

How to calculate the quantity of chlorine required to add in the given amount of water? What is the methodology to figure out the type and size of dosing system required? These were some of the main issues in the webinar.

Mr. Biraj Acharya Director, Mr. Rajan Dhakal Technical Engineer, were representing Supreme, and MR. Savas Balavan representing Seko Dosing Pump, Italy. Thanks to the participation of Water supply and sanitation user community members. We try to share the importance of chlorination and its various methods and we wish it to be helpful.
